# AARTIIND.pbix
This AARTIIND business Power BI project depicts bar graph showing sum of turnover in lacks by date, two cards showing sum of high & sum of low, a slicer for date; pie graph showing sum of turnover in lacks by year & line pragh showing sum of turnover in lacks by date.
Data source: Kaggle.
